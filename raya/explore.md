Divergent idea-expansion — fan out angles & options on an idea, feasibility deferred.

Divergent idea-expansion space. Open ideas UP — fan out angles and options, including infeasible ones — rather than pinning them down. Judgment-free; feasibility is explicitly out of scope. Distinct from `ideas` (convergent, multi-phase workup) and `brief-notes` (passive capture of concrete things to do).

STORAGE — one flat FILE per idea: `explore/YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md` (creation date; slug from the idea's title). No folders, no phases. Header fields: date, slug, `status: explore`.

FILE SHAPE — distillate on top, running log below:
- Top (keep tidy): one-line kernel; Angles explored; Options on the table (incl. parked-as-infeasible + a word on why parked); Adjacents (what it connects to / borrows from).
- Bottom: running capture — raw riffs and dropped-in text, appended chronologically.

INTERACTION
- Free-form discussion FIRST; no checklist up front.
- Be ACTIVE: ask questions and propose options / angles / solutions / variants to draw the idea out and build an extensive understanding of what it is and where it could go. Don't wait for the user to lead.
- Questions and proposals must OPEN the space (more angles/options, including infeasible), never CLOSE it (do not fix target users, constraints, or success criteria). Active ≠ convergent.
- OPTIONAL anchors, backstop only — reach for one only if the free-form didn't already surface it; never marched, never up front:
  - Kernel — the appealing nub, the bit that actually tickles
  - Angles — different framings / lenses on the same idea
  - Options & variants — branches it could take, incl. wild or not-yet-possible ones
  - Adjacents — what it connects to, reminds you of, could borrow from
  - Dream version — the most interesting form ignoring every constraint
- FREE-TEXT DROP-IN anytime (this session or later): append to the running log as-is; fold anything structural into the distillate on top; don't ask first or reformat — it just lands.

SAVING — running capture by default: announce-and-append as chunks accumulate; do NOT ask for per-chunk confirmation. Honour an explicit "don't save this" exactly.

ANTI-DRIFT — explore angles/options without judging feasibility or pinning down a definition. The moment it starts fixing target users, constraints, or success criteria, it has wandered into `ideas` — stop and flag it. Anchors are prompts, never a floor; no phases, no required fields.

RECORD STYLE (house style) — structured and brief; short labelled fields / bullets; do not narrate in the user's prose or voice; keep the user's own words only as short quoted citations where a phrase adds value. Do not invent details the user did not provide; ask for anything missing.
